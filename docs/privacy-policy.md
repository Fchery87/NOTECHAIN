# Privacy Policy

**Effective Date:** February 8, 2026

## Introduction

NoteChain ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our encrypted note-taking service.

## Our Privacy-First Approach

NoteChain is built on **zero-knowledge encryption** principles:

- We cannot read your notes or content
- Encryption happens on your device
- Only you hold the keys to decrypt your data
- We collect minimal data necessary to provide the service

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

**Account Information:**

- Email address (for authentication and communication)
- Password (hashed, never stored in plain text)
- Display name (optional)

**Payment Information (Pro users):**

- Processed by Stripe (we never store full credit card numbers)
- Billing address (if provided)

### Information We Don't Collect

We specifically **do not** collect:

- Content of your notes
- Encryption keys
- Recovery keys
- Note metadata that could reveal content
- Browsing history
- Search queries

### Automatically Collected Information

**Usage Data (anonymized):**

- App feature usage (e.g., "note created", "AI used") - without content
- Performance metrics
- Error logs (without personal data)

**Technical Information:**

- Device type and browser
- IP address (temporary, for rate limiting only)
- Timestamps of API requests

## How We Use Your Information

1. **Provide the Service:** Authentication, sync, backup
2. **Improve the Service:** Anonymous usage analytics
3. **Communication:** Account notifications, security alerts
4. **Security:** Fraud prevention, abuse detection
5. **Legal Compliance:** When required by law

## Data Storage and Security

### Encryption

- All user content encrypted with AES-256-GCM
- Encryption keys generated and stored on user devices
- Transport encryption using TLS 1.3

### Data Location

- Primary servers: EU (GDPR compliant)
- Backups: Encrypted, distributed across multiple regions
- CDN: Global for fast access

### Retention

- Account data: Retained until account deletion
- Deleted notes: Soft deleted for 30 days, then permanently removed
- Logs: Anonymized after 90 days

## Your Rights (GDPR)

If you are in the European Union, you have the right to:

1. **Access:** Request a copy of your data
2. **Rectification:** Correct inaccurate data
3. **Erasure:** Delete your account and data
4. **Portability:** Export your data
5. **Restriction:** Limit processing
6. **Objection:** Object to processing
7. **Automation:** Not be subject to automated decisions

To exercise these rights, email: privacy@notechain.app

## Cookies and Tracking

### What We Don't Use
